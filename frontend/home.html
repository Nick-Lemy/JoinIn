Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to commonly asked questions about JoinIn.

How do I create my own event?

To create an event, log in to your JoinIn account, click on the "Host an Event" button on the homepage, and follow the guided process to set up your event details.

Can I join events outside my department?

Absolutely! JoinIn is designed to foster cross-departmental collaboration. You can browse and join any public event on the platform.

How do I get support for my event?

JoinIn offers event management support for official university events. Contact our team through the form above or email support@joinin.alu.edu for assistance.

Is JoinIn available for alumni?

Yes, JoinIn is available to current students, faculty, staff, and alumni of African Leadership University. Alumni can access the platform using their ALU email address.
